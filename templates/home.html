About the system

Why BISU Balilihan built a test bank

The BISU Test Banking System replaces scattered folders, printed questionnaires, and filing cabinets with a single digital archive. Faculty can submit, tag, and retrieve exam items in seconds, and every item passes through a review step before it becomes part of the shared bank — so what's stored is always accurate and ready to use.

It's designed around three roles: administrators who oversee the whole campus repository, sub-admins who manage their department's collection, and teachers who contribute and draw from the bank for their own subjects.
