How school years work:

• The current school year is the active one — admins assign subjects to teachers under this year.

• When you set a new year as current, teachers' subject assignments are not automatically cleared — you manage assignments manually in the Manage Teachers page.

• Past year assignments are preserved as read-only history and can be viewed in the Manage Teachers page using the year switcher.

• You cannot delete the current school year.
