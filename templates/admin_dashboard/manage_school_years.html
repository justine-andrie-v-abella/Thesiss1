How school years & semesters work:

• Each school year can have up to two semesters. Only one semester system-wide is current at a time — activating a semester also marks its school year as current.

• Admins assign subjects to teachers under the current semester only.

• Past semester assignments are preserved as read-only history and can be viewed in the Manage Teachers page using the semester switcher.

• You cannot delete the current semester or the current school year.
